## 1. HOW TO PULL TODAYS TRENDING AUDIOS (15-Min Playbook)

Since trending audios change weekly or even daily, here is the exact process to get the freshest data right now:

### Step 1: TikTok Creative Center (3 min)
- Go to: **https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/inspiration/popular/music/pc/en**
- Filter by: **Region** (United States / United Kingdom), **Time** (Last 7 days or Last 30 days)
- Sort by: **Popularity** or **Usage growth**
- This gives you the official, data-backed top trending sounds with exact usage counts

### Step 2: TikTok Search Bar Method (5 min)
- Open TikTok app > Search
- Type each of these queries and look at top results:
  - hopecore
  - betting on myself
  - founder journey
  - new chapter
  - startup life
  - here is my story
  - motivational monologue
  - cinematic transition
- Tap on videos with high engagement > tap the spinning vinyl/audio icon at bottom right > see how many videos use that sound

### Step 3: Check Trend Aggregators (5 min)
- **Tokboard.com** - Real-time trending sounds dashboard
- **TrendTok app** (iOS) - AI-powered trend predictions
- **Toktrends.com** - Categorized trending audio lists
- **Reddit: r/tiktoktrends** and **r/tiktokhelp** - Community-sourced trending sounds threads

### Step 4: Check Creator Economy Newsletters
- Later.com/blog
- SocialInsider.io/blog
- HootSuite blog
- Epidemic Sound blog
- Kat Norton / Miss Excel (regularly posts trending audio roundups)

## 2. TRENDING AUDIO ARCHETYPES FOR LOGARA CONTENT

These are the recurring audio **types** that go viral cyclically on TikTok. Even when the specific song changes, the archetype persists. This is your strategic layer.

### A. HOPECORE / MOTIVATIONAL

| Archetype | Description | Fits Content Type |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Inspirational speech over cinematic music | Voiceover from movie/podcast/speaker layered over epic orchestral or piano music | Story, Reflection, Depth |
| Gentle reassurance | Soft-spoken affirmations over ambient/lo-fi music. Extremely shareable | Reflection, Depth |
| Triumph montage audio | Builds from quiet/sad to triumphant. Users show where I started vs where I am now | Story, BTS, Reflection |
| Nobody believed in me narrative | Spoken word about proving doubters wrong, paired with beat drops | Story, Trend |
| Cinematic piano with text overlay | Instrumental only; users add their own text. Works for silent storytelling | Story, Reflection, Depth |

**Why this matters for Logara:** Hopecore consistently outperforms other audio categories for engagement because it triggers save + share behavior. It aligns perfectly with founder journey content.
